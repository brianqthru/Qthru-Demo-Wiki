QTHRU POS DEMO WALKTHROUGH FOR iOS DEVICES

 

 

 

After completing your account and store account on the QThru Retailer Portal. Copy to your iPad your QThru POS URL located at the bottom of the Store Details page of the QThru Retail Portal

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Type your QThru POS URL into the address bar of your iOS device. Wait for the webpage to load and install the web app on your device. Note the on screen instruction showing you how.

 

 

    

 

 

 

 

 

And then click “Add to Home Screen”. Located in the center.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

And then click “Add”.

 

 

  

 

 

 

 

An icon will be placed on the your device. Double click to open.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Sign in with your QThru Retailer Portal login and password.

 

 

    

 

 

 

 

 

You should now be in Kiosk mode and ready for scanning.
